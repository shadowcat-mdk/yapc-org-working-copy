Keyword Replacement

Template files may contain some keywords in capital letters which are automatically replaced with corresponding values when the pages are created. The most important are 


  PAGE
  TITLE
  URL_PREV
  URL_NEXT
  URL_INDEX

See the --bottom_template option and the --top_left_txt option for more information.
